SECTION B - THEORY QUESTIONS (51–63)
Answer all questions. Show all steps clearly.

51. The time taken to buy fuel at a filling station varies directly as the number of vehicles $V$ in a queue and inversely as the number of pumps $P$ available at the station. In a station with 5 pumps, it took 10 minutes to fuel 20 vehicles.
a) Determine the relationship between $t, P,$ and $V$. Represent the solution as $t = aV/P$, where $a$ is an integer. Find $a$.
b) Calculate the time it takes to fuel 50 vehicles at a station with 2 pumps.
c) Find the number of pumps required to fuel 40 vehicles in 20 minutes.

52. A car traveled a distance of $(2x + 13) \text{ km}$ at $67.5 \text{ km/h}$ and $(5x - 20) \text{ km}$ at $72 \text{ km/h}$. If the total time for the journey was 90 minutes, find the value of $x$.

53. A circular floor is to be tiled with ceramic tiles each of side $40 \text{ cm}$. If the perimeter of the floor is $66 \text{ m}$, calculate, to the nearest whole number, the number of tiles required to completely tile the floor. (Take $\pi = 22/7$)

54. In the diagram, there is a circle with center $O$. Quadrilateral $OBCD$ is a rhombus such that $\angle ADO = \angle OBA = y$ and $\angle BAD = t$.
a) Find the value of $t$.
b) Find the value of $y$.
c) Calculate $\angle ADC$.

55. A basket contains 3 gold-plated marbles, 4 diamond marbles, and some silver marbles, all of the same size and shape. Two marbles are drawn one after the other without replacement. If the probability that both marbles are silver is $1/15$, find the number of silver marbles.

56. Given that $(x + 2), (4x + 3), (7x + 24)$ are consecutive terms of a geometric progression (G.P.):
a) Find the value of $x$. Represent the answer as $a, b, c, d$, where $a$ is negative and $b, c, d$ are positive integers.
b) Determine the common ratio of the G.P.
